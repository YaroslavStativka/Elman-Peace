SPORTS 4 DEVELOPMENT

We know movement, well-being, and sporting activities like football can have incredible impacts on the healing of young people following devastating life events.

We know that sophisticated and inspired curricula developed and applied in conjunction with the acquisition of new skills can change lives and have catalytic effects within communities struggling with poverty, crime, conflict and inequality.

Football

It continues to run this peacebuilding campaign by providing schooling and a route to earning a living without carrying a rifle for tribal warlords or violent extremist groups.

Basketball

We also know the potential of this field is not fully tested in Somalia due to a lack of prioritization of sports for development over the many other needs of the fragile population.

There are no “quick fix” solutions for Somalia’s many problems. There are, however, means which can facilitate the process and thus contributing to community development and peace-building. We refer here to sports!
